FAQ

We accept .csv and .txt files with one phone number per line. Numbers can include dashes, parentheses, or spaces — we normalize them automatically.

1 credit = 1 phone number, regardless of how many scrub types you select. A file with 10,000 numbers costs 10,000 credits to process.

Most jobs complete in under 2 minutes. Larger files (>500K numbers) may take up to 10 minutes. You'll receive an email when your results are ready.

Uploaded files and result files are retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted. Download your results promptly.
Terms of Use
By submitting a scrub job you confirm that: (1) you have a legitimate business reason for scrubbing these numbers, (2) you will not use results to contact numbers on the DNC registry, (3) results are for your internal use only and may not be resold.
